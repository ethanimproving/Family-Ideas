# Ingredients

Chicken Breast (26 oz.)
Kidney Beans (15 oz.)
Pinto Beans (53 oz.)
Black Beans (30 oz.)
Rotel (11 oz.)
Diced Tomatoes (14.5 oz.)
Taco Seasoning Mix  (3 tbsp.)
Ranch Dressing Mix (3 tbsp.)
Whole Kernel Corn (22 oz.)

# Directions

1) Open all cans
2) Put everything into large pot on medium flame
3) Stir occasionally
