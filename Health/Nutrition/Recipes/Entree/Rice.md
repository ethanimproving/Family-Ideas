# Ingredients

1 cup Rice
1.5 cup Water
1/2 tbsp. Butter
1 tsp. Salt
Garlic Powder

# Directions

1. Rinse rice
2. Pour rice in pan
3. Pour water in pan
4. Wait until lid dings
5. Turn heat off and wait another 15 minutes
